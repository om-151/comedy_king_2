Comedy has undergone a remarkable transformation over the years, evolving alongside changes in technology and societal norms. Today, the digital landscape provides a vast canvas for comedians, YouTubers, and content creators to experiment with various comedic styles. From short skits to elaborate sketches, the possibilities are endless. In the age of memes and short attention spans, micro-comedy has become a dominant force. Snappy, bite-sized humor in the form of memes, GIFs, and short video clips often spreads like wildfire across social media. These quick hits of comedy provide instant gratification and serve as a digital antidote to the stresses of everyday life.
